Why I Love It

Pickleball found me on a sunny Saturday in 2022 and never quite let go. It rewards patience, footwork, and a certain mischievous love for placing the ball exactly where your opponent isn't. Below are the three things that hooked me — short list, big feelings.

Community

Show up to any open-play session and you'll leave with three new friends and at least one nickname.

Strategy

It looks casual until you realize every dink, drive, and drop is a chess move with a paddle.

Accessibility

You can pick it up in an afternoon and still spend a lifetime trying to master it.

The Rules (in 60 seconds)

Here's the cliff-notes version. The ordered list is the flow of a point — the bullets nested inside are the gotchas that catch beginners.

  1. Serve underhand and diagonally cross-court.
    • Both feet behind the baseline
    • Paddle contact below the waist
    • The ball must land in the diagonal service box
  2. Let the ball bounce once on each side (the “double bounce rule”).
    • Returner lets the serve bounce
    • Serving team lets the return bounce
    • After that, volleys are fair game — except…
  3. Stay out of the kitchen on a volley.
    • The 7-ft non-volley zone is sacred ground
    • You may enter to play a bounced ball, but get out before you volley
  4. Score only when serving. First to 11, win by 2.
